BBQ Sandwhich

Ingredients

  1. 2 Chicken Breast
  2. Bacon
  3. Sub Roll
  4. American Cheese
  5. BBQ Sauce
  6. Lettuce
  7. Tomato

The Process

  1. Preheat oven to bake on 350Â°F.
  2. Cook Chicken for 15 mins.
  3. At the end of the 15 mins glaze chicken with BBQ, continue cooking for 5 mins.
  4. While Chicken cooking make the bacon.
  5. After everyting is finished cooking toast sub roll with american cheese on it.
  6. Add chicken and bacon onto sub and finsh off with more BBQ sauce. Optional add lettuce and tomato.
