Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  1. Sriracha
  2. Blue Cheese Crumber
  3. Cream Cheese
  4. Ranch
  5. Mozzeral Cheese (Shreaded)
  6. Canded Chicken

The Process

  1. Preheat oven to bake on 450Â°F
  2. Pour 1 cup of siracha in to bowl
  3. Add 1 stick of Cream Cheese
  4. Add 1/2 cup of Blue Cheese Crumble
  5. Pour 1/2 cup of raunch dressing into bowl
  6. Mix the canned chciken into the bowl
  7. Bake for 10 mins and mix then bake for another 10 mins

The Buffalo Chicken Dip is a great appetizer for game night, a party, or any type of gathering. This dip is usally server with chips, my personal preference is Totitos scoops to ensure a heavty amount of dip. Other options for dipping includes but not limited to, pretzels (sticks), other corn based chips or tortia based chips. This dish can be quite SPICY because of the siracha, use as much as you feel fit to handle.
